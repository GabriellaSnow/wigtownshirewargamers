About Us

Welcome to the Wigtownshire Wargamers — a community of tabletop strategy enthusiasts passionate about Warhammer 40,000, Age of Sigmar, Horus Heresy, Kill Team, and other miniature wargames. Whether you're a seasoned commander or brand new to the hobby, you're welcome at our table. We're not all about warhammer though, no no! We also love to dive into board games, tabletop RP such as D&D and Pathfinder, and card games like Magic: The Gathering.

Upcoming Events

Club night!

Date: Every second and fourth Sunday of the month

Location: Millenium center, 75 George St, Stranraer, DG9 8DX

Join us for another evening of gaming, laughs, and good company! Whether you’re into miniature wargames, board games, or tabletop RPGs, there’s always something on the go. Bring your armies, dice, or favourite game—or just come along and join in! New players are always welcome, and there’s usually a mix of casual and narrative games happening each week..

Join Our Community

Interested in joining? We welcome players of at least 18+ at all experience levels. Drop in at any event or send us a message to get started. We can't wait to meet you!
